
# Kishan Hub

## About The App
Android Studio application built for ICL India, offering a dedicated Agricultural Video-on-Demand (VOD) platform for both mobile phones and Android TV.
The app provides a personalized user experience with curated collections of vegetables, fertilizers, fruits, and additional agricultural content, allowing farmers and technicians to easily browse and watch relevant videos.

## Technical Information
Developed in Android Studio with a modular architecture.
Integrated multiple Firebase services for authentication, real-time data management, remote configuration, and analytics.
Included Google AdMob for ad-based monetization, and implemented HoneyGain and MoneData as external monetization SDKs.

The application has been published on the Google Play Store for several months, reaching over 10,000 downloads and averaging around 600 daily active users
